FAQ

Explore common inquiries and insightful answers in our FAQ section, designed to provide clarity and support for your healthcare journey

How to book appointments online?

You can schedule appointments online by logging into your account and selecting an available time slot.

Can appointments be rescheduled?

Yes, you can easily reschedule or cancel appointments through the online system at your convenience.

Is my information secure?

Rest assured, we prioritize the security and confidentiality of your personal information through advanced encryption measures.

What if I encounter technical issues?

If you experience technical issues, please reach out to our support team for prompt assistance.

Are there additional features available?

Yes, our online system offers features such as appointment reminders and follow-up options to enhance your experience.

How far in advance can appointments be booked online?

You can book appointments online up to 24H, providing flexibility for your scheduling needs.

How can I make payments for appointments?

You can make payments in person at the clinic using cash, or other accepted methods.

Can appointments be rescheduled or canceled online?

Yes, you may arrange payment via bank transfer or other remote methods as instructed by the clinic.

Is my information secure?

No, there are no additional fees for alternative payment methods at our clinic.
