Excel Labs Tests

Excel Lab was established in 1991 and today it is considered one of the largest private laboratories in Pakistan. We are offering quality services 24/7 all across Pakistan. It's been 31 years since Excel Lab has been serving people through its best diagnostic services. Our staff is trained to treat our patients as the center of care, our customer service is available to ensure that any query and concern are catered to efficiently. Excel Lab offers a wide variety of tests including all lab tests, CBC, Cholesterol tests, Blood Sugar Tests, Urine Tests, and many more.  

Excel Labs has aimed to build a service that people trust and that doctors can rely on when prescribing treatment. Over the years, we have led the way in preventative healthcare and diagnostics and created a more aware citizenry conscious of its health and well-being. We at Excel Labs strive to spread this light to all corners of the country and play our role in building a healthy and happy Pakistan.
